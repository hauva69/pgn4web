  1. enter your PGN text in the large textbox below
  2. modify any of the options
  3. generate the HTML code and preview the iFrame with the action button
  4. tune options, especially the frame height/width (some browser might render some items slightly bigger or smaller)
  5. cut & paste the iFrame HTML code in your web page or your blog
  6. email to pgn4web@casaschi.net the address of your website or your blog
